This project is a Chrome extension inspired by GoFullPage, aiming to support full-page screenshot capture, scrolling capture, cropping, editing, and exporting.

## Highlights

- Planned browser-side screenshot stitching and image editing workflow.
- Motivation: building a free and customizable alternative for full-page capture and cropping.

## Context

The project focuses on a compact browser-side workflow: capture the page, stitch the result, make common edits, and export without relying on heavyweight external tools.
